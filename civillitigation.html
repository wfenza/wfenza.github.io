Contracts

Contracts are open to a wide amount of interpretation, and there are numerous judicially-created contract interpretation methods. The services of an experienced attorney are crucial in determining the meaning of contract terms. Whether you need a contract drafted or you are involved in a dispute over the meaning or fulfillment of a contract, give me a call.

Employment Discrimination

Numerous federal and state laws prohibit discrimination in employment on the basis of race, color, religion, sex (including pregnancy), national origin, age, disability or genetic information. If you have been discriminated against or harassed on the basis of a protected characteristic, call me today to discuss your legal options.
