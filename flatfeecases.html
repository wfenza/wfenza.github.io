Transparent Pricing

Flat Fee Cases

Most misdemeanors (in Pennsylvania) and disorderly persons offenses (in New Jersey) can be handled on a flat fee basis. All flat fees include two court appearances. Additional court appearances will require additional payment. Accepting cases on a flat fee basis is discretionary and is not guaranteed to any potential client.


New Jersey Criminal Matters

  • Minor moving violations (speeding, unsafe lane change, failure to present documents, etc.)- $500
  • Major traffic offenses (driving while suspended, reckless driving, etc.) - $1,000
  • Petty disorderly person offenses - $1,500
  • Disorderly persons offenses - $3,000
  • D.U.I - $3,000

Pennsylvania Criminal Matters

  • Minor moving violations (speeding, unsafe lane change, failure to present documents, etc.)- $500
  • Major traffic offenses (driving while suspended, reckless driving, etc.) - $1,000
  • Third-degree misdemeanor - $1,500
  • Second-degree misdemeanor - $2,500
  • First-degree misdemeanor - $3,000
  • D.U.I - $3,000
