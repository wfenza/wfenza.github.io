Practice Areas

  1. Personal Injury
    1. If you have been injured, you may have a right to sue. In all personal injury matters, you pay no attorney fees unless you actually recover money.* Consultations are always free, so contact me today to discuss your legal options.
      *clients are responsible only for fees and actual expenses associated with litigation. See Pricing for details.
    2. Motor Vehicle Accidents
      1. If you have been involved in a motor vehicle accident, you may be entitled to compensation. Both Pennsylvania and New Jersey allow individuals to recover for medical expenses, damage to a vehicle, and, under certain circumstances, pain and suffering. Contact me today for help understanding what options you have and what steps we can take together to make sure justice is done.
    3. Other Injuries
      1. There are a wide variety of circumstances which potentially could entitle you to compensation. Slip and falls, unsafe premises, construction accidents, and intentional injuries are just a few examples of the type of incidents which regularly entitle people to compensation. No matter your injury, contact me today and we can work together to discover whether your injury may entitle you to compensation.
  2. Family Services
    1. Divorce
      1. Divorce is a fact of life in the modern world, and trying to navigate the legal system on your own can be confusing. Don’t end up with an unfavorable settlement because you didn’t understand the process. Speak to me today, and together we can work out what to expect, what you should be doing right now to protect your interests, and where to go from here. Whether you’re already divorced, you’re in the middle of a divorce, or you are thinking of filing for divorce, give me a call and let’s see if I can help you out.
    2. Nontraditional Family Services
      1. Sometimes, families don’t quite fit the usual mold, and legal marriage is not always the best structure for a person’s relationship(s). Call me today and we can discuss legal options other than marriage for establishing rights in a relationship, sharing property, establishing custody of children, and planning for the future, no matter what happens.
    3. Other Family Services
      1. If you would like to change your name, draft a will, revisit a custody arrangement, modify a child or spousal support order, establish a prenuptial agreement, or if you have any other family court matter, please contact me today for legal help and advice.
  3. Civil Rights
    1. 42 U.S.C. § 1983 provides that the U.S. Government may not violate your rights under the U.S Constitution. Courts have interpreted this statute to entitle individuals to sue the United States for any violation of an individual’s Constitutional rights. Pennsylvania and New Jersey both have similar statutes. If you feel that your rights have been violated by government actors, you may be entitled to money damages. Contact me today to find out if you have an actionable claim.
  4. Criminal Defense
    1. D.U.I.
      1. Penalties for driving under the influence of a controlled substance can be thousands of dollars, loss of driving privileges, or even jail time. If you have been charged with D.U.I., don’t go into court alone. Call me today to discuss the strategy for your defense.
    2. Traffic Offenses
      1. Traffic violations in Pennsylvania and New Jersey can have serious consequences. Tickets can result in hefty fines, points on your license, loss of driving privileges, or jail time. It pays to have an experienced advocate in your corner. Contact me today to discuss how I can help with your traffic offense.
    3. Other Criminal Matters
      1. If you have been accused of any crime, please do not hesitate to contact me. I am able to help with charges such as drug possession, assault, theft, or other charges. In addition, I maintain a network of top-quality criminal attorneys, so if I am not able to help with your matter, I will find someone who can.
  5. Civil Litigation
    1. Commercial Litigation
      1. In business, misunderstandings can happen, and disputes are inevitable. Having a skilled attorney on your side is invaluable when it comes to a business dispute. Call me today to discuss potentially solutions to your business dispute.
    2. Contracts
      1. Contracts are open to a wide amount of interpretation, and there are numerous judicially-created contract interpretation methods. The services of an experienced attorney are crucial in determining the meaning of contract terms. Whether you need a contract drafted or you are involved in a dispute over the meaning or fulfillment of a contract, give me a call.
    3. Employment Discrimination
      1. Numerous federal and state laws prohibit discrimination in employment on the basis of race, color, religion, sex (including pregnancy), national origin, age, disability or genetic information. If you have been discriminated against or harassed on the basis of a protected characteristic, call me today to discuss your legal options.
    4. Other Civil Litigation
      1. Lawsuits can take many forms, and cover a myriad of issues. If you are thinking of filing a lawsuit or you are already involved in one, give me a call to discuss your options going forward. As always, if I am not able to help you with your issue, I will find you someone who can.
